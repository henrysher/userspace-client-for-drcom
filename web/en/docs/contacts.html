Contacts


Have a question , or need help ? There are many people in the community who can assist you.

How to contact


  • User list

    • There is no technical support by telephone. Most questions are answered in our Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ), Manual, and the Tutorials and Tips on our Wiki.

    If you still need help, expert assistance is available on our User List. To post a question, register, confirm your registration then log in. Click "New Topic" top left, then submit your message. Languages are available for English and Chinese.

    To give us feedback that does not require a personal response, please e-mail us in English or Chinese. Please tell us your experiences of the Drcom-Client program, documentation or web site. We particularly welcome reports of possible program bugs and requests for new Drcom-Client features.

  • Mailing list

    • Subscribe to our Mailing List to discuss Drcom-Client with our community of users and developers. Join our developers' list to learn about the Drcom-Client source code and contribute to making Drcom-Client even better!

  • Bug Report

    • When reporting a problem or apparent bug to the Tickets, please include full details of what you are trying to do. If there is a crash or error message, please state what you did that led up to this, and what exactly any error message said. For more information, see Bug Report.

  • Chat on IRC

    #drcom-client on Freenode
